SHINee

Is a five member group that debuted on May 22nd, 2008. Each member possess many different talents. They all do solo projects outside of the group. They star in various dramas, movies, commercials and ads. Together, they visit different variety shows together. They have been together for one year less than a decade, which is a rarity in KPop groups. They treat one another like family, and try their hardest to support one another in their separate adeventures.

Together they have:

  • Has spent the last decade together
  • They have eleven studio albums
  • They have thirty-five music videos
  • 59 awards
  • 5 world tours
  • Members Minho and Key didn't get along for a period of three years because of a misunderstanding.
  • They love In and Out Burgers.
  • Earned the title 'Princes of KPop'
