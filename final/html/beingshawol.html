Being a Shawol

  1. You must always support SHINee!
  2. You will love and cherish each member respectfully!
  3. You will not bash any member for any reason.
  4. Yes, you are allowed to have your own opinions, but it can not contradict with the last two rules.
  5. You will listen to their music
  6. You Will watch their music videos.
  7. You WILL eat, sleep, breathe SHINee
  8. You will know their Korean, non romanized names.
  9. When you meet the members of SHINee, you will greet them as "Thank you, oh great 'member's name' for giving me this opportunity to be in your presence."
  10. You will thank the staff for helping SHINee, and for protecting the members.
  11. You will thank the member's families for giving them life and helping raise them into whom they are today.
  12. During award shows, you will support SHINee to the fullest, whilst giving respect to other KPop idols and groups.
  13. If you can't attend a SHINee concert, a link will be provided to you as a live stream. You must watch the live stream.
